# Grinding Operations

## Overview

Grinding operations generate flying particles, sparks, metal dust, and high noise levels. A hazard assessment must be completed before work begins.

## Required PPE

- Safety glasses with side shields
- Face shield
- Hearing protection
- Cut-resistant gloves
- Safety footwear
- Flame-resistant clothing when spark exposure exists
- Respiratory protection when airborne dust exceeds exposure limits

## Main Hazards

- Flying particles
- Sparks
- Metal dust
- Noise
- Eye injuries
- Hand injuries

## Best Practices

- Inspect grinding wheels before use.
- Ensure machine guards are installed.
- Secure the workpiece.
- Do not use damaged grinding discs.
- Keep flammable materials away from sparks.

## Important Note

Always follow the manufacturer's instructions and site-specific procedures.

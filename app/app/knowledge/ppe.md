# Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)

## Overview

Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) is equipment worn to minimize exposure to workplace hazards that can cause injury or illness. PPE is the last line of defense and should always be used together with engineering and administrative controls.

## Common PPE

- Safety Helmet
- Safety Glasses
- Face Shield
- Hearing Protection
- Respiratory Protection
- Protective Gloves
- Safety Footwear
- High Visibility Clothing
- Flame Resistant Clothing
- Fall Protection Equipment

## General Requirements

- PPE must be selected based on the identified hazards.
- PPE must be inspected before each use.
- Damaged PPE must be removed from service immediately.
- Employees must receive training on proper PPE use.
- PPE must fit the user correctly.

## Best Practices

- Always perform a hazard assessment before selecting PPE.
- Replace damaged or expired PPE immediately.
- Keep PPE clean and properly stored.
- Follow manufacturer instructions for maintenance and replacement.
